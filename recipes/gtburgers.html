Ground Turkey burgers

Description

A ground turkey burger is a type of burger made primarily from ground turkey meat, which is a leaner alternative to ground beef. The patty is usually formed from a mixture of ground turkey (often a blend of white and dark meat for added juiciness), seasonings like garlic, onion, salt, pepper, and sometimes breadcrumbs or egg as a binder.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ground turkey
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Pickles
  • 1 egg (optional, for binding and moisture)

Steps

  1. Make the Turkey Patty Mixture
  2. Form the Patties
  3. Cook on the Stovetop
